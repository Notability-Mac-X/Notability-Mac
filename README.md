## Overview

Notability Professional Suite is a complete audio-synced note-taking platform that serves students and professionals who need their written notes and recorded audio to function as a single navigable record.

Multi-note linking and subject organization group related notes by class or project, keeping a semester or ongoing project's worth of audio-synced notes structured and easy to revisit.

PDF annotation tools bring the same handwriting and audio-sync capability to imported documents, letting recorded discussion of a document stay attached to the specific pages and passages it concerned.

Notability Professional Suite is the platform for anyone whose note-taking happens alongside spoken audio, and who wants both halves of that record to stay permanently connected.

## Performance & Integration

Notability is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Notability processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
